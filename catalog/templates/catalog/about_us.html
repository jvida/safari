About Us

Wilderness MANIAC SAFARIS is a tourism company with its headquarters in Arusha, Tanzania. It operates in the east of Africa and is owned by a young Tanzanian entrepreneur Floridi Ishmael Laisser.

Floridi is a young Maasai, born and raised in the community surrounding Tarangire National Park, where his family has worked his whole life. Floridi has undertaken his primary and secondary education in local schools of Arusha. In 2003, he started his career in tourism industry by working as a porter in Mount Kilimanjaro for more than three years. Mount Kilimanjaro, the highest free-standing mountain in Africa, was the place where he managed to get sufficient money to continue his studies and later, he got the Tourism and Wildlife Management Certificate at the Arusha Institute of Travel and Tourism Management. Afterwards, he started working as a professional tour guide for high rated tourism companies.

Lastly, Floridi has taken another step in his career with the decision to start his own tourism company to enhance young people of Tanzania in the tourism industry and he also wants to play a role in promoting tourism in Tanzania and Africa at large. The word MANIAC in the name of the company revealing his obsession and absolute passion for exploring the wilderness of the world, as well as showing these wonders to the tourists.
