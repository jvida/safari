Accommodation

We offer a variety of accommodation which will definitely fulfill your expectations. You stay at lodges and tented camps depending on your preference. All accommodations have electric lights, battery recharging facilities, some have Wi-Fi and swimming pools.

Spending a night in a tent under the starry sky surrounded by wild animals expressing themselves with a large variety of different noises can be a magical experience which doesn't fit everyoneÂ´s demand. For those who don't delight in camping there is also another option- safari lodge. It is a more luxurious and comfortable way of spending your night, in a room with your own bed, shower etc.
Depending on the time of arrival and departure you either spend a night in a hotel or not. We will inform you in advance and choose accommodation you would like.

Food

During the whole trip there will be a professional chef ready to cook whatever he see in your eyes. In the beggining he would ask you wether you eat meat/fish or not. After obtaining the informations he needs he would prepare menu that would suit your needs. Breakfast- pancakes/omelets, boiled eggs, cheese, butter, bread, marmalede, coffe, tea, fruit Lunch- rice/pasta, meat/vegeterian dish, vegetable, fruit Dinner- rice/pasta, meat/vegeterian dish, vegetable, fruit
